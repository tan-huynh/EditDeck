**OVERALL TEMPERAMENT & PHILOSOPHY**
The presentation embodies intellectual rigor meeting corporate clarity. Every visual decision serves information transmission, not aesthetic indulgence. The aesthetic is "quiet confidence"—subtle sophistication that commands attention through precision rather than decoration. Think laboratory whiteboard meets modern consulting firm. The visual language whispers authority while remaining accessible. There is no playfulness, no whimsy, no emotional manipulation through color. The design is a vessel for complex information, not a performance.
**LAYOUT SKELETON & ARCHITECTURE**
Vertical rhythm follows a 48px base unit. All vertical spacing (margins, padding, line height) is a multiple of 48px or its fractions (24px, 16px, 12px). This creates mathematical harmony across the deck.
**GRID SYSTEM & WHITESPACE**
The grid is the presentation's skeleton. Every card, panel, and content block aligns to these 80px columns. Gutter space between columns is 20px. Horizontal spacing between sections is 64px (1.33 units). Vertical spacing between slides in a sequence is 96px (2 units).
Whitespace is active design, not empty space. Margins between text blocks are 24px. Margins between cards are 48px. Margins between section headers and content are 96px. This breathing room prevents cognitive overload. The eye travels smoothly because there are no visual obstacles—only clear pathways between information clusters.

Secondary background: Very light gray (#F8F9FA) used only for:

- Footer areas
- Sidebars (if used)
- Chart backgrounds (when needed for contrast)
Tertiary background: Dark charcoal (#333333) reserved for:
- Footer text
- Small decorative elements

No gradients. No patterns. No noise. No subtle textures. The background is a blank canvas that lets content breathe.
